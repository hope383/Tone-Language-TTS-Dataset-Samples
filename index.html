本文展示了针对壮语、瑶语、泰语、越南语及缅甸语构建的高质量TTS语料样本。
All corpora were recorded in professional studios with 48kHz sampling rate.

缅甸语语料(男生)
Sample 1

Sample 2

Sample 3

Sample 4

Sample 8
